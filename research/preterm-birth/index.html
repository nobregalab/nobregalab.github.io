Genetic mechanisms of preterm birth

Like many human traits, premature birth is a combination of environmental risk factors and genetic predisposition. By examining the genetic risk factors associated with preterm birth, we can identify genes whose mis-regulated expression increases the risk that an invididual person will give birth prematurely.

Our lab has generated gene expression data, epigenomic data, and chromatin interaction data from placental stromal cells. Using these data, we are identifying novel factors which maintain pregnancy.
